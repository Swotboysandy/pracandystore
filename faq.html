Frequently Asked Questions

How does Cash on Delivery work?

Cash on Delivery (COD) means you pay for your order when it arrives at your doorstep. No advance payment needed.

How long does delivery take?

We deliver within 3–5 business days, depending on your location. You’ll get a tracking link after placing your order.

Are prices cheaper than Amazon/Flipkart?

Yes! We offer lower or competitive prices and no hidden fees. What you see is what you pay.

Can I cancel my order?

Yes, you can cancel your order before it's shipped. Contact us with your order number as soon as possible.

What if I receive a damaged product?

Returns or refunds are possible only after checking and verifying the issue. Please email us within 24 hours of delivery.

Do I need to create an account to shop?

No, you can shop as a guest without creating an account.

What payment methods do you accept?

We currently accept only Cash on Delivery (COD) across all cities.

Do you ship to all locations in India?

Yes, we ship to most cities and towns across India. Remote locations may take longer.

How can I contact support?

You can email us directly at info.pracandystore@gmail.com.
