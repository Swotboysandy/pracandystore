Frequently Asked Questions

How does Cash on Delivery work?

Cash on Delivery (COD) is a payment method where you pay for your order when it arrives at your doorstep. Simply select COD during checkout, and our delivery partner will collect the payment when delivering your order. No advance payment is required.

How long does delivery take?

We typically deliver orders within 3-5 business days. Delivery time may vary depending on your location and product availability. You can track your order status through the order confirmation email.

Are prices cheaper than Amazon/Flipkart?

Yes! We offer competitive prices on all our products. We regularly compare our prices with major e-commerce platforms to ensure you get the best deals. Plus, with no hidden charges and transparent pricing, you always know exactly what you're paying for.

Can I cancel my order?

Yes, you can cancel your order before it is shipped. To cancel, please contact our customer support team with your order number. Once the order is shipped, cancellation may not be possible.

What if I receive a damaged product?

We thoroughly check all products before shipping. However, if you receive a damaged product, please contact our customer support within 24 hours of delivery. We'll arrange a replacement or refund as per your preference.

Do I need to create an account to shop?

No, you don't need to create an account to shop with us. You can place orders as a guest. However, creating an account allows you to track orders and access exclusive deals.

What payment methods do you accept?

We currently accept Cash on Delivery (COD) as our primary payment method. This allows you to pay when you receive your order, making shopping more convenient and secure.

Do you ship to all locations in India?

Yes, we ship to all major cities and towns across India. Delivery time may vary depending on your location. Some remote areas might have longer delivery times.
